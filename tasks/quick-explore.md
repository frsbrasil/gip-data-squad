# Task: Quick Explore

## Trigger
User wants a fast dataset overview without a full pipeline.

## Steps
1. Profile the dataset (shape, dtypes, nulls)
2. Show top 5 rows
3. Distribution of numeric columns
4. Top values in categorical columns
5. 3 most interesting observations

## Delegate to
ega-agent only.

## Output
Concise EDA summary (< 1 page) with 1-3 charts.
